🥽 MosMedAR

Moscow Medical Augmented Reality — Intelligent visualization for education and image interpretation

MosMedAR is a research project developing mobile AR applications for medical education and training. We create accessible, scalable, and evidence‑based AR tools that improve spatial anatomy understanding, medical image interpretation (CT, MRI, ultrasound), and hands‑on skills on physical phantoms.

📱 Our AR Applications

🟢 Active development · RCT ongoing

🧪 PhantomAR

AR visualization of multimodal medical images (CT, MRI, ultrasound) over physical phantoms. Designed for anatomy education and radiology training.

thyroid phantom head phantom abdominal phantom Unity + Vuforia
Status: Active development · RCT in preparation (Q3‑Q4 2026)
Target journal: Computers & Education (Q1, IF ~10.5)
🟢 Active development · Prototype ready

📊 DICOM‑X

AR DICOM viewer with multiplanar reconstruction (axial, sagittal, coronal). Visualize real patient CT/MRI series in augmented reality space.

DICOM parser multiplanar crosshair sync Android
Status: Prototype ready · Technical validation ongoing
Target journal: Medical Image Analysis (Q1, IF ~8.0)
